About Me

Howdy! My name is Ethan Partin, I am a Junior at Texas A&M University where I am in the process of earning an undergraduate degree in Computer Science as well as pursing a minor in Business.

Hobbies I enjoy include basketball, hanging with my friends, and straight boolin.

Outside of class I serve as the Alumni Liaison for Lambda Chi Alpha where I am a Founding Father for the Delta Mu colony here at Texas A&M University. The work I have done with the fraternity has helped me grow as a leader; from planning events along with their respective budgets, to presenting at meetings, to maintaining professional communication with our alumni.

In order to balance school, work, and my extra-curricular activities, I have had to rely on my strong work ethic as well as master my people and time management skills. No matter the task, no matter my experience, I am always willing to jump on board and tackle any challenge head first.
