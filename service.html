Service

Throughout my entire life I have spent time doing community service. When I was younger this was the work I would do with Boy Scouts, and now in my collegiate career it has shifted to work I do with my fraternity, Lambda Chi Alpha. Our current philanthropy is the Bryan Animal Shelter and this September we are also putting on a concert to support the Bryan Police Department called Back the Blue 2019. Being in a position where I can help out others is a humbling and special exprience, and I look forward to my continued community service.
